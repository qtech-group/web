## About Us

QTech Group is a leading construction firm specializing in civil and building construction, project management, and plant supply across Africa. With over 10 years of combined industry experience, we pride ourselves on delivering quality projects on time and within budget.

## Our Vision

To be a leading construction firm in Africa and to empower our staff with the necessary skills to adapt to changing industry demands.

## Our Services

### Core Offerings
- 🏗️ **Civil Construction** - Expertise in infrastructure and civil engineering projects
- 🔧 **Installing Services** - Professional installation services for various construction needs
- 📋 **Project Management** - End-to-end management of construction projects
- 👥 **Subcontractor Management** - Coordination and oversight of specialized subcontractors
- 🪨 **Aggregate Supply** - Provision of quality construction aggregates
- 🚜 **Plant Supply** - Supply of construction machinery and equipment

## Our Objectives

1. Provide excellent project management teams
